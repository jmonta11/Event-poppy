Pop on over to Poppy Fest!

Take a tour to California's famous eye-popping golden poppy fields

Information

Welcome to the Poppy Festival!
We hope you enjoy and make some fun memories, right here in Lancaster, California. There are so many fun things to do at the festival! The weather is sunny and is also perfect for sightseeing the glorious golden fields of California.

Directions

The California Poppy Festival is held at Sgt. Steve Owen Memorial Park (formerly Lancaster City Park), conveniently located just off the 14 freeway at Avenue L.


If you have any questions about the festival, or if you'd like to volunteer, please contact us.
